Brand assets for digital product development

Basic Pearson brand assets in one place

Get all brand resources you need on start of digital products development and save your time for searching and writing simple code.

Technology Independent

Use with web and native apps. Color palettes and mixins are available in LESS, SASS, Stylus, and XML. Quarks don’t depend on any libraries or frameworks.

Use it now

Get visuals assets from CDN or download them and host by yourself. Don’t waste your time for graphics and font format conversion
