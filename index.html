Logo

The primary version of our logo should be used wherever possible. Where the primary version is not appropriate, e.g, in digital environments or where there is restrictive space, we also have a horizontal version.

Color

Color brings a design to life. Color is versatile; it's used to express emotion and tone, as well as place emphasis and create associations. Color should always be used in meaningful and intentional ways in order to create patterns and visual cues.

Typography

Our brand typefaces are Playfair Display and Open Sans. Using both serif and sans serif typefaces gives us the flexibility to cover our wide range of audiences and communications.
